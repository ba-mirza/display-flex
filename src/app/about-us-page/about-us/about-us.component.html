About project

KazMed is part of the largest Internet holding in Kazakhstan, Chocofamily. The holding's projects include Chocolife, Chocofood, Chocotravel, Rakhmet and Lensmark.

The mission of the Chocofamily projects is to increase the number of happy people. We improve their quality of life, saving time and money, allowing them to travel more, discover the world and spend time with their loved ones. KazMed is an online service for finding and making appointments with doctors in Kazakhstan.
On the website you can:
- make an appointment with a doctor of interest;
- get an online consultation without visiting a doctor;
- leave a review about the doctor you visited.
